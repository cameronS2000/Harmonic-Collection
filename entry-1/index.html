iPhone Photography and It's Place in its Field

the convenience of mobile phone cameras forever changed the process and accesibility of photography.

realism and artistic value

As photography became an available technology to society, the value of realism in the arts became less relavent.

As a result, artists became more interested in abstraction.

This change in job specification allowed the artist to focus on the general aspects of their pieces like composition, color, and subject.

I believe a similar movement is taking place in photography.

accessibility and excess

Nearly every member of society having access to a camera at all times means that every moment can be captured.

This allows for a similar abstract freedom that was presented to artists in the mid 1800s

This makes room for the appreciation of pixelated, over-exposed, or blurry photographs.

This website will be used to express my appreciation for the ability of my iphone and the new era of photography it has created.
